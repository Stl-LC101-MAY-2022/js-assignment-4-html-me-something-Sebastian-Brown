My Favorite Music!

What Genre?

I like all genres of music, however, rock and heavy metal are by far my favorite. That being said, some of my favorite songs are from different genres like pop and country.

My Favorite "Rock/ Heavy Metal" song!

I have listened to rock/ heavy metal more than any other genre of music. It is by far my favorite genre. As for my favorite I'd have to say it would be The Unforgiven III by Metallica.

My Favorite Pop Song!

Pop was definitely still really big when I was a kid, so I heard a lot on the radio. My favorite pop song would have to be Down by Jay Sean.

My Favorite Country Song!

I used to hate country music with a passion. However, in an effort to have more things to connect with my friends on I bit the bullet and started listening. Over time it grew on me and I can definitely enjoy some country song and can recognize the appeal of most. As for my favorite country song, that would be Chicken Fried by Zach Brown Band.

© WMG

My Favorite Rap Song!

Last but not least, we get to rap. Rap is another genre that I never really explored until my friends told me I should. Today it's on of my favorite genres. My top rap song is 5 Pack by Jady.
